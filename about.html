About Scrummer

Scrummer was created to help Scrum Masters and delivery teams interpret sprint metrics more intelligently.

After years of working in Agile environments, I observed a recurring pattern: metrics were often used without context, leading to confusion, pressure, and sometimes misplaced blame.

Scrummer aims to change that by:

  • Providing realistic forecasting support
  • Highlighting risk early
  • Explaining metric trends clearly
  • Encouraging system-level thinking instead of individual blame

Built with practical Agile experience and a strong focus on clarity, Scrummer is evolving into an intelligent sprint interpretation tool for teams of all sizes.
